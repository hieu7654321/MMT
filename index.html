DIJKSTRA SIMULATR

Welcome to the Dijkstra Simulator, an interactive tool designed to help you explore and understand the Dijkstra algorithm for finding the shortest paths in a graph. Whether you're a student, educator, or enthusiast, our simulator provides a hands-on experience to visualize how the algorithm navigates through nodes and edges to determine the most efficient routes. Input your own graph data, set your starting point, and watch as the algorithm calculates the shortest paths in real-time.
